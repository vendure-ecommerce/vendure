The Settings section covers configuring your Vendure store.

## Global Settings

Configure store-wide settings such as available languages, default language, and inventory tracking options. See [Global Settings](/user-guide/settings/global-settings) for details.

## Channels

Manage sales channels for multi-storefront or multi-currency setups. See [Channels](/user-guide/settings/channels) for details.

## Administrators & Roles

Manage administrator accounts and define roles with specific permissions. See [Administrators & Roles](/user-guide/settings/administrators-roles) for details.

## Countries & Zones

Configure countries and group them into zones for shipping and tax purposes. See [Countries & Zones](/user-guide/settings/countries-zones) for details.

## Taxes

Configure tax categories and tax rates for different regions. See [Taxes](/user-guide/settings/taxes) for details.

## Shipping Methods

Define shipping methods and their eligibility rules. See [Shipping Methods](/user-guide/settings/shipping-methods) for details.

## Payment Methods

Configure available payment methods for your store. See [Payment Methods](/user-guide/settings/payment-methods) for details.
