The Orders section covers managing customer orders in your store.

## Orders

View, search, and manage orders placed by customers. Handle payments, fulfillments, refunds, and order modifications. See [Orders](/user-guide/orders/orders) for details.

## Draft Orders

Create orders manually on behalf of customers, useful for phone orders or special requests. See [Draft Orders](/user-guide/orders/draft-orders) for details.
